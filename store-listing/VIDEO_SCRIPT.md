# Promo video outline

Chrome's published listing instructions request a YouTube promo-video link. The
dashboard wording may vary, but this outline keeps a recording short, accurate,
and consistent with the submitted extension.

## 30-45 second recording

1. **Problem (5 seconds):** Show a YouTube discovery surface containing one or
   more retailer-purchase trend videos. Do not show private account information.
2. **Filter (10 seconds):** Enable Sieved, refresh or navigate within YouTube,
   and show that matching discovery cards are gone. Open the toolbar popup and
   show its hidden-card count.
3. **Control (15 seconds):** Open More settings. Briefly show the retailer and
   trigger toggles, then the custom-term and channel-allowlist controls.
4. **Privacy (5 seconds):** End on the settings page with the on-screen caption:
   "Local filtering. No ads, telemetry, or remote requests."

Suggested title: **Sieved for Chrome - filter repetitive YouTube videos**

Suggested description: **A short demonstration of Sieved 1.0.0. Sieved filters
matching YouTube discovery cards locally using user-configurable rules.**

Use the same icon and colors as the store assets. Avoid claims that every
matching video will be detected or that unrelated YouTube content is blocked.
